Topnji History


Topnji Construction has been in operation since 2000, serving NY, NJ, TX and surrounding areas. We have successfully completed over 10,000 projects, ranging from small service work orders to multi-million dollar renovations, as well as new construction. Topnji Construction employs a unique and diverse set of skilled professionals that range from project managers and field specialists to mechanics from our large number of partnered companies. We have a long list of satisfied clients that include large governmental entities to small private companies.  You can view the quality of our work which is displayed in our Completed Projects gallery.




Topnji’s vision, core values, and guiding principles enhance the organization’s internal culture, and maintains Topnji’s reputation as a construction leader, an employer of choice, and an active community member.

Topnji aspires to be the most respected builder, renowned for: Excellence Leadership Unsurpassed value CORE VALUES  Honesty Topnji employees are open, candid, and truthful. Integrity Topnji employees’ word is their bond. They do what they say, and live up to the highest standards of fairness and ethical behavior. Respect Topnji employees work hard to earn business partners' trust and respect on every project they undertake. Dynamic Culture Topnji employees constantly seek new opportunities to learn, to improve, to teach, and to add value. Passion Topnji employees love what they do, taking the lead, and leading by example. GUIDING PRINCIPLES Ownership The Topnji family of companies is 100-percent employee-owned. That ownership means extraordinary performance by employees and outstanding value for clients. At the same time, local ownership ensures that local dollars remain in the communities where Topnji employees live and work. Teamwork Every employee and business partner has something to contribute to Topnji's construction teams. This belief is the key to mutual success. Mutual Obligation In return for adding value in the business, Topnji employees can expect to perform meaningful work and to have satisfying careers. Safety Topnji will not compromise the health and safety of its people. Effective Communication Topnji provides the accurate and timely information required to support good decision-making. Diversity Topnji values men and women of diverse ages, religions, and ethnic backgrounds. Mobility Topnji’s geographic diversity is an asset. Social Responsibility Topnji encourages and supports its employees in their desire to improve the quality of life in their communities. Topnji is a contributing partner to the development of the construction industry through active involvement in its associations.
